Our Story

We both relocated to Dubai the same week and at this time had no idea that our paths were destined to cross. Our first date was one of our most memorable ones. We met in a nice restaurant early in the afternoon overlooking the beautiful Dubai Marina and from there we spent the whole day together, getting to know each other deeper and discovering more about each others personalities. The hours went by so quickly and we ended the day along the beach in the evening and by then had already planned our second outing this time in Abu Dhabi. The whole day was so much fun, conversations flowed effortlessly, there was never a dull moment. We realised the perfect connection we had but unaware that this was just the very beginning of our exciting journey.

The Proposal —
Her Story

I honestly wasn't expecting it the day he proposed. We decided last minute to have a yacht party to celebrate his 30th birthday and make it special. We organised the gathering with all our friends and after the song 'African Queen' came on he got down on one knee and popped the question! Mark is a funny, kind and considerate man who won my heart and I thank God for our journey so far. I look forward to many more years ahead x

His Story

By our second date I knew she was the one. From the beginning we clicked. Her smile, she's down to earth and so loving. The time came when I knew I was going to propose but didn't know how or when. I found the perfect ring for her and worked with one of my close friends to keep it a secret. Now I was just waiting for the right moment and the perfect opportunity came about when she planned my birthday. I'm so glad everything worked out perfectly.
